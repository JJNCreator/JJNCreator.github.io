Mythical Freaks

Mythical Freaks is a 2D fighter made in Unity. In the game, you play as a cryptid and fight other cryptids. I did all the programming while outsourcing the art and music to a couple college classmates.



Contributions

Gameplay mechanics, character animation, managing version control, UI, platform deployment, localization

Software used

Unity, Visual Studio, Photoshop, Git, Inkscape, XCode, Android Studio, Amazon CodeCommit

Technical challenges

Some of the issues I ran into were getting the animations to work properly. I also had trouble with setting up server-side receipt validation, though it's more secure than local validation.

Lessons learned

One thing I learned is art can be difficult to make iterations for, especially when it hasn't been updated in a while. I also learned that fighting games can be tough to perfect.

Future work

Local multiplayer, better animations, more characters and stages, finishing moves, cheat codes, AI improvements, and save system optimizations
